about us

Kano State Secondary Management Board(kssmb) also known as gidan Malamai(Teacher house) is the organization saddled with the responsibility of employment,training,monitoring and management of government teachers and secondary schools across kano state.

this is the headquarters and it has branches in different educational zones within the states.the zones oversee the secondary schools within the various local government under their jurisdiction.

it is a large organization headed by and appointed executives sectary and assisted by several directors who are carrier civill servernt that grew through the ranks.
